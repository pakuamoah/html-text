PARAGRAPHS

A Paragraph consists of 1 or More Sentences that form a self-contained unit of discourse. The start of a paragraph is indicated by a new line.

Text is easier to understand when it is split up into units of texts. For example, a book may have Chapters. Chapters can have Sub-Headings.Under each heading, there will be one or more paragraphs.
